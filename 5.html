Don't Move

“Don’t you dare move, or else…”, one of the four holding you down says as your friend raises the cane.
You close your eyes, accepting your grim fate and waiting for your blood to start trickling off your backside and onto the floor.
WHACK!
There was the cane, but there was no pain. Instead, you hear a deep and hoarse groan despite it being almost inaudible. “ES STURCUS!” Mr. Arduum shouts in a hoarse voice again. (literally, “you excrement!”) Apparently, your friend “missed” and hit Mr. Arduum in the shins instead.
“I will not suffer this nonsense!” Mr. Arduum shouts as soon as he recovers from the blow.
Mr. Arduum grabs the cane from your friend and grabs you by the neck with one hand and swings the cane with his other hand.
WHACK!
You fight back tears as the cane lands on your rear end. It was just a ¼-inch-thick wooden stick wrapped with thorned vines, but it was exceptionally painful for whatever reason today. Maybe they used a different type of vine? Maybe the weather is colder than usual? You think mockingly as the cane lands again, this time on your bare back… just a few more to go, you think to yourself as you feel your own warm blood trickle down your backside onto the floor…
