Introduction

Time: 30 BCE
Place: Rome, Italy
Your Mission: Survive school

You are Tiberius Claudius Nero, the adopted son of Augustus Caesar. You are now three months into your last year of grammaticus (grammar school), after which you will pursue rhetoric and politics as your career path (but that’s another story which happens around 6 years later). In order to do so, you will need to live through grammaticus, i.e., grammar school, which (by the name) is something too dark and twisted to comprehend… good luck, and STAY OUT OF TROUBLE!!!
