Silent

You fall silent. Mr. Arduum smirks at you as you look down.
“I thought so… disappointing. Now look here, boy. You might be the future emperor, for all I care. Yet, as of now, you are my student, and I am your teacher, Mr. Nero. I have absolute authority over whatever happens in this class, and even if you run home scared telling your father what had happened today, he cannot do anything to me.”
There was a dreadful silence in the classroom as everyone looks at the floor. You would never be discouraged by such an insignificant person, and an even more insignificant remark (though there must be a bit of truth in it -- either that, or Mr. Arduum must be a very professional con artist.)
