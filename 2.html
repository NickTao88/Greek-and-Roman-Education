LATE

“Mr. Arduum, you’re late to class! You were supposed to be here half an hour ago!”
You yell down the courtyard. Mr. Arduum wheels around and points at you sternly, shouting,
“THOU SHALT NOT PASS!” (This is just Mr. Arduum’s way of telling people to stop right there…)
“Mister…” Mr. Arduum began, only to falter in calling you by name.
“Tiberius… sir,” you answer hopefully, preferred to be called by your first name.
“Right… you insolent son of the devil. You were supposed to be here 45 minutes ago! Remember the class rules?”
Mr. Arduum continues, ignoring your helpful remark. “As such, by the rules, you will receive 5 strokes of the cane. Now hurry up and enter the classroom, or it will be 39.”
Mr. Arduum walks off with his head held high as you follow behind trudgingly. (39 strokes of the cane was the maximum punishment allowed under Imperial law.)
